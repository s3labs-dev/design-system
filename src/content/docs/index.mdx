## Design Philosophy

The s3 labs design system embodies **Solarpunk Synthesis** — a vision where technology grows from natural systems rather than opposing them. Our visual language draws from:

- **Deep Forest Foundations** — Dark, organic backgrounds that feel alive
- **Bio-Luminescent Accents** — Green, cyan, and gold that glow with purpose
- **Spectral Flow** — Motion captured in stillness, velocity encoded in gradient
